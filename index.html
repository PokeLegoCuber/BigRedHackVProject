What is Robby Rescue Robot?

Robby Rescue Robot is a platform that can teach a community how to find signs that of a disaster and how to go about preventing or reacting to it. One of the educational games that we made taught basic fire prevention, safety, and escape.

Toaster Fires

Toasters fires can start when toaster ovens malfunction. More commonly, they ignite bread/grain particles accumulated at the bottom of the tray. If a fire were to start, first unplug the toaster. Then try to smother the fire. If unable to contain the fire, escape the building and call 911.

Candle Fire
